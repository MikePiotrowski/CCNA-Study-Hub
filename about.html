About My CCNA Journey

Documenting my path to Cisco Certified Network Associate certification, one subnet at a time.

Background

I'm Michael Lee Piotrowski, an aspiring network engineer passionate about understanding how the internet works at a fundamental level. My journey into networking began with curiosity about how data travels across the world in milliseconds, and that curiosity has grown into a dedicated pursuit of the CCNA certification.

After exploring various IT domains, I found my calling in networking—the backbone of modern technology. The CCNA represents not just a certification, but a comprehensive understanding of network fundamentals that power everything from small home networks to global enterprise infrastructures.

Study Goals

  • Master Core Concepts: Deep understanding of routing, switching, IP addressing, and network security
  • Hands-On Practice: Build confidence through Packet Tracer labs and real hardware configuration
  • Pass CCNA 200-301: Target certification date: Summer 2025
  • Career Transition: Land a network engineering role in a growing tech organization
